⚔ The Guardian Must Rise ⚔
◆

Hear me, young scholar of the coordinates, for the fate of our beloved Willowmere rests in your hands alone.

For three hundred years, the great monster guardian Vexathar has slept beneath Grimstone Peak, held in ancient slumber by a magical seal. Vexathar is a tremendous beast — part dragon, part wolf, part ancient magic — and it exists for only one purpose: to protect Willowmere from the Shadow Creepers that dwell in the Hollow Mist Forest to the north.

But last night, the Hollow Mist began to move. The forest crept three miles closer to town. Mayor Gamal rang the great iron bell at midnight — the first time in a century. The Shadow Creepers are coming, and they will arrive at dawn in two days.

🔮

There is only one way to summon Vexathar: the Guardian Map. This enchanted crystal map, hidden in the town vault, requires a precise drawing to awaken — the exact shape of Vexathar must be traced using coordinates on a magical grid. Only then will the guardian rise.

The last cartographer who knew the coordinates vanished in the year of the Great Fog. But we found her hidden notebook — a list of (x, y) coordinate pairs, plotted in precise order, that trace Vexathar's true form. Plot every point. Connect them in order. Do not skip a single coordinate.

The Shadow Creepers tremble at one thing alone: the roar of Vexathar. You must summon the guardian before dawn.

Will you plot the Guardian's form and save Willowmere?

The Cartographer's Field Notes
🗺 Reading the Magical Grid
◇

The Guardian Map uses a coordinate grid. Every point on this grid has an address written as (x, y) — two numbers that tell you exactly where to mark the map.

➡️

The X Coordinate

X tells you how far right to go. Start at zero (the left edge) and count right until you reach the x number.

⬆️

The Y Coordinate

Y tells you how far up to go. Start at zero (the bottom edge) and count upward until you reach the y number.

📍

The Order Matters!

Connect each new point to the last. Some sections must be drawn separately — the clue will tell you when to "lift your quill."

◆

Example: The point (5, 3) means → go 5 spaces right, then 3 spaces up. Mark it! ✦

Vexathar's form is made of five mystical sections — the body, the tail, the wings, the horns, and the fiery eye. Each section will be plotted with a different colored line on the Guardian Map. When all sections are complete, the guardian will be revealed!

    🗝 Teacher Answer Key

    The Guardian of Willowmere — Coordinate Plane Activity

    TEACHER ONLY
    About this activity: Students plot 49 coordinate pairs across 5 sections to reveal Vexathar, a fantasy guardian creature. The grid spans (0,0) to (20,20). Students must connect points within sections in order, and lift their quill between sections. Two sections (Wings and Horns) have sub-paths requiring mid-section lifts.

    📋 Suggested Grading Rubric

    ScoreDescriptionAccuracyBadge
    100 / A+ All 49 points plotted correctly on first try with no hints used. Guardian fully revealed. 100% Master Cartographer
    90–99 / A All points plotted correctly. Minor errors self-corrected without hints. 85–99% Guardian Scholar
    80–89 / B All points completed. Used 1–3 hints. Some coordinates needed multiple tries. 70–84% Apprentice Mapper
    70–79 / C Most points correct. Used 4+ hints. Pattern mostly recognizable. 55–69% Novice Plotter
    Below 70 Struggled with axis identification or ordered pairs. Recommend additional practice. Below 55% Needs Practice
    Learning Objectives: CCSS 5.G.A.1 — Use a pair of perpendicular number lines (axes) to define a coordinate system. Understand that (x,y) describes a location using the x-axis (horizontal) and y-axis (vertical). Plot and connect coordinate pairs to form geometric shapes.

    Common Errors to Watch For: (1) Reversing x and y axes — student plots (y,x) instead of (x,y). (2) Counting from top-left instead of bottom-left. (3) Off-by-one errors near origin. (4) Not connecting points in order within a section.
